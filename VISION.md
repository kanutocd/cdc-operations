# CDC Operations Vision

## Mission

Define the operational vocabulary for CDC systems.

## Principles

- Platform-neutral
- Documentation-first
- Operationally boring
- Observable
- Predictable
- Composable
- Cloud-agnostic

## Philosophy

CDC systems have operational characteristics that differ from traditional request/response applications.

Those characteristics deserve explicit contracts independent of infrastructure.

## Long-Term Vision

Become the operational foundation for CDC workloads regardless of deployment platform.
